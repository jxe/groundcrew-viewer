Welcome to the Groundcrew Interactive Demo

The people to the right are fake – you can message them and they’ll automatically respond.

Here’s some things to try…

  • Click people on the map, or in the facebar above, to see their profile and send a message or mission invitation.
  • From a profile, click tags to automatically search for more people with the same skills or interests.
  • In the facebar at the top of the screen, people are grouped by availability.These groups can be collapsed and expanded. Send someone on a mission to see their status change.
  • You can also start missions at other locations from the gray menu bar at the bottom of the screen.
  • The little photos on the map are landmarks. Click one and run a mission with the people nearby
