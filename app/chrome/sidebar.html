Here's how to get started…

1. Send yourself a test message

Click on yourself in the top bar (or on the map – either works). Type a message and hit 'Send'.

You'll receive it as a notification if you've installed the iPhone app, or as a text message for other phones.

2. Invite new agents

Click on 'Recruit' down at the bottom. You can email invitations or share (via Twitter, Facebook, etc.) a link to a sign-up page.

Agents show up in the upper-right (you should see yourself there, too). Click on them to see them on the map.

3. Try your first mission

Try this on yourself if there are no other agents…

If location matters most: create a new mission by clicking the Send menu, then choose 'A Mission'. You'll choose where the mission happens, and people nearby will be invited.

If who's chosen matters most: click on people (in the top bar or on the map), then give them a mission.

You'll see all your in-progress missions from the 'Ongoing' menu at the bottom.

4. Gather feedback from the group

Send out a question or a message to everyone (from the 'Send' menu)

5. Hide this sidebar

Just off the upper-right corner of this sidebar is a button. That will hide (and show) this sidebar.
